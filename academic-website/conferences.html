Data Geography

The longitudinal datasets behind the research

This research draws on major longitudinal and cross-national survey infrastructures. Each dataset defines its own geographic footprint, independent of where the analysis itself was presented or taught.

SHARE

SHARE — Survey of Health, Ageing and Retirement in Europe

Cross-national panel covering 27+ European countries, used across the retirement, functional-health and wellbeing research stream.

HILDA

HILDA — Household, Income and Labour Dynamics in Australia

National household panel survey, used for research on functional decline, aged-care use, hope and wellbeing among older Australians.

ALSWH

ALSWH — Australian Longitudinal Study on Women's Health

Longitudinal cohort study used to estimate the costs of age-related care needs among Australian women.

JLMPS

JLMPS & Jordan DHS/JPFHS

Jordan Labor Market Panel Survey and Demographic and Health Survey data, used for the development-economics research stream on household decision-making, early childhood development and domestic violence.
