Applied Economist · Adelaide, Australia

Economic research that connects data, policy and people.

I am an applied economist working on labour economics, the economics of ageing, education and human capital, development, and policy evaluation. My identification strategies recover causal effects from longitudinal survey data, for research, policy, and practice.

Academic research, university teaching, and commercial economic analysis, drawing on large-scale longitudinal datasets: SHARE, HILDA, ALSWH, and JLMPS.

Introduction

A research agenda grounded in applied econometrics

PhD economist with applied econometrics and quantitative modelling experience across academia and industry. I build economic models — fixed effects, IV-2SLS, difference-in-differences, regression discontinuity — to answer policy-relevant questions, and I write the results for the audience in front of me: academic, government, or commercial.
