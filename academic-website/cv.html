Profile

PhD economist with six years of applied econometrics and quantitative modelling experience across industry and academia, including a commercial role as an Economic and Energy Market Analyst at Mubadala, Qatar. Builds economic and econometric models — fixed effects, IV-2SLS, difference-in-differences, regression discontinuity — in Python, R and Stata to answer policy and commercial questions, and translates the results into clear, decision-ready visual narratives through dashboards, teaching and conference presentations. Currently leads Agile, DevOps and Design Thinking training for the Innovation Academy in Digital Business, delivered in partnership between Accenture and Adelaide University. Full Australian work rights; available for Canberra-based work.
